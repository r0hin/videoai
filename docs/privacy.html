1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Information

- Photos: The App requires access to your device's photo library to use as input for creating videos.
- Email Address/UserID: We collect your email address or userID exclusively for user authentication purposes.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- Device Information: We may collect information about the device you are using, including but not limited to the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use, the content you create, and your interactions with the App.

1.3 Third-Party Data Collection

Photo data may be sent to ReplicateAI for AI purposes. This data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information. ReplicateAI may store the data for up to 60 minutes for processing and analysis. After this period, the data is permanently destroyed.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Creating Videos

The primary purpose of the App is to use AI to transform user photos into videos. The photos provided by users are processed locally on the device, and no user-generated content is stored on our servers.

2.2 ReplicateAI

To enhance the AI capabilities of the App, photo data may be sent to ReplicateAI for analysis. This data is used exclusively for improving the AI model and is subject to ReplicateAI's privacy policy.

2.3 Video Generation Results

The results of the video generation process are stored indefinitely until the user deletes their account. These results are accessible by the user only.

3. Data Storage

The App utilizes Google's Firebase for data storage. Please note that data stored in Firebase may be subject to Google's privacy policy. In some situations, Google may have access to the stored data as outlined in their privacy policy.
